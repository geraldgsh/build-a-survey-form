Coworking Space Survey

A Coworking Space is a shared work space for entrepreneurs, telecommuters, small businesses or anyone needing office space, that provides desks, computers, office equipment, high speed internet, conferencing rooms and equipment for rent at a more economical rate than renting or buying office space.

Typically, co-working spaces use a monthly (or longer) subscription or rental model (participants are often called members). Some offer drop-in or short-term memberships. Still others offer classes, training, workshops and equipment rental.
